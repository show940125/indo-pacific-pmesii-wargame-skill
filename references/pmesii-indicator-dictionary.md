# PMESII Indicator Dictionary

- `P` Political: policy cohesion, alliance signaling, institutional responsiveness
- `M` Military: force posture, mobilization indicators, deterrence balance
- `E` Economic: trade continuity, sanction pressure, fiscal resilience
- `S` Social: public sentiment, civil stability, strategic narratives
- `I` Information: information contestation, narrative penetration, media pressure
- `Infra` Infrastructure: logistics continuity, critical node resilience, cyber-physical readiness

Indicator tiers:

- `leading`: early warning signals (typically I/M)
- `significant`: strong directional signals (typically E/Infra)
- `confirmatory`: trend confirmation (typically P/S)
